At the core of every business, there needs to be profit margins when selling a product. This is where this comes in hand. Whether or not you are a new business starting out or a long time business with lots of sales, this calculator can be used to calculate how much you are really making per sale. You would first want to enter in your total sales and after expenses have been taken into account, enter in the profit which would be (total sales - expenses) which would equate to your profits. Enter those two values in and you get your net margin or approximately how much you make per sale!
